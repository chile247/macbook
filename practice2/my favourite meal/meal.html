Yamarita

About the Food

Yamarita is plain boiled yam that is coated in egg, flour, ginger, garlic, and salt. Donâ€™t forget the frying part. This is one of the most popular Hausa food. I see it in a different restaurant every day, it is nearly as popular as suya. This recipe is detailed and one of the most comprehensive.Yamarita is just easy and delicious. You can make this in just about 45 minutes. I decided to serve with tomato stew. You can also serve with egg sauce, ketchup or even Monica sauce.

Ingredients and Measurement
Measurement ingredient
3 slices Yam
2 cups of flour Flour
4 eggs eggs
1 dice of onion onion
1/2 teaspoon of sugar sugar
1 cup of vegetable oil Vegetable oil
2 fresh diced pepper Pepper
2 table of milk Milk

Steps/ Methods used in preparing Yamarita

  1. Cut the Yam
  2. Wash and boil the yam with little salt
  3. Mix flour with milk and sugar
  4. Whisk the egg into the bowl with flour and sugar
  5. Add little water and stir until it is creamy
  6. Dip the boiled yam in the mixture
  7. Put in hot cooking oil to fry
  8. Serve while hot
